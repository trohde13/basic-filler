Introductions

Hello My Name Is:

Hi! My name is Thomas Rohde and in addition to working as a Solutions Engineer at Queue-it, I also write and paint. Generally, I've been writing horror fiction because I've found that many stories can be improved by adding monsters. I also write about film, pop culture, and doing art stuff at my blog. I tend to use watercolors when I paint, though I started teaching myself acrylics a few years ago. I also draw. My influences come from many areas (film, books, comics - naturally) & a variety of creators (writers & artists). If you want to know more, just ask!

Stuff & Things:

I like to travel with my wife and hang out with our pups: Ava & Oscar. Some of our favorite trips have included: Paris, Iceland, a road trip to Colorado. My wife is a photographer who really kills it with portraits of pets and professional headshots.
